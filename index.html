About Battle Lands

Battlelands is a project based on the excellent development of Blockchain Technology and the NFT game. It is also an attractive battle royale game in which players can earn tokens through a life-and-death battle with other players.
In Battlelands, you must be a survivor in battle with other players. Try to loot weapons and items that dropped around the map to survive. Playing alone or team up (Solo/Duo/Squad) makes your game experience more fun and competitive.
Play Battlelands game and manage own NFT assets in the blockchain economy, and can easily swap to real MONEY.

Game Play

You will be shipped to battle land to fight with other battlers. You can play with solo/duo/squad mode with a mission to become the last survivor. A death ring circle will surface on the map’s borders and will slowly shrink, limiting the area in which fighters can fight. Beat all of them to become the ultimate survivor and claim your prizes.
MOBA (5vs5), future feature: Players will divide 2 teams with the purpose of fighting each other. The team with the most kills will be the winner.
Custom Battle: Players can create battles with their own rules to play with their friends.

Free to play & Invest to earn

  • Buy characters or character boxes to own paid characters to battle and earn tokens.
  • Sell NFT items on the marketplace to make profit.
  • Clone characters from your own base character after that transfer to other players on the marketplace.
  • Character rental (future feature): Players can lease their Fighters to other players at a predetermined price. When players have many unused characters, they can supply it to other people to rent out and get money.

OWN LANDS

In the future, it would be great if you could own your own land. Are you looking forward to that? With Battlelands, that will come true when you can own your land and rent it out to other players.
Buy land to create your own virtual world and generate revenue.
